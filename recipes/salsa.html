Fresh Tomato Salsa

Yet Another Mexican Favourite

This is a delicious homemade salsa.

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • ½ cup finely diced onion
  • 5 serrano chiles, finely chopped
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice

Steps

  1. Chop all ingredients
  2. In a medium bowl, stir together tomatoes, onion, chili peppers, cilantro, salt, and lime juice.
  3. Chill for one hour in the refrigerator before serving.
